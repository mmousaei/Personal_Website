Bio

I am Mohammadreza Mousaei. I was born and raised in Tehran, Iran. I am fascinated by the world of science and technology. Traveling is my motivation when I'm busy working; and playing (Guitar) and listening to all kind of music (especially Rock) is my favorite activity at my spare time.

I received my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from Shahid Beheshti University. Thereafter, I worked at Sharif University of Technology and Amirkabir University of Technology as an assistant before I move to US to start my masters. I received my M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from University of Illinois at Chicago. I'm currently a Robotics researcher at Field Robotics Center of Carnegie Mellon University under supervision of Prof. William (Red) L. Whittaker

My research interests includes Robotics, Computer Vison, Machine Learning as well as Information Theory and Wireless Communication. To see my full academic profile, you can download my CV or visit my Google Scholar profile.
